A Brief Background

In 2009 when Bishop Justin Alain was a young man, one time when he went to pray at the church in the village where he lived, there was a youth training program with the theme of: Let your gift shine if it is Jesus who gave it to you. Wherefore I put thee in remembrance that thou stir up the gift of God, which is in thee by the putting on of my hands. For God hath not given us the spirit of fear; but of power, and of love, and of a sound mind. Be not thou therefore ashamed of the testimony of our Lord, nor of me his prisoner: but be thou partaker of the afflictions of the gospel according to the power of God. (2 Timothy 1:6-8)

The training was organized by people from America. After three days of training, it made him understand that it was time for his talents to be revealed and enlightened to many. It motivated him to use the gift that given to him by God.

Since that time, he felt that there are people who are self-deprecating among them who feel that they are not capable of anything, he feels that he should go and tell people to show their talents because they were given to them by God.

Years later he got married and continued the good work of serving God through the gifts given to him by God together with his wife in expanding the kingdom of God. Around 2012, God found Bishop Justin Alain in a vision.

In that vision, God showed him 5 stars in 1 star and immediately produced 3 Things, God has revealed to him that the five stars are the 5 directions of the earth, the five continents of the world, and the 5 directions that characterize man if they will combine in 1 star (Jesus Christ) would come out:
