A Brief Background

It all began with Justin Alain and a young man, one time when he went to pray at the church in the village where he lives, there was a youth training program with the theme of 'Let your gift shine! If it's Jesus who gave it to you, show Him'...

The training was organized by people from America. After three days of training, a youth from umudugudu was given time for his talents to be revealed and enlightened to many. I motivated him to use the gift God gave to him by God's spirit to praise and worship God...
